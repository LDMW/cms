Free mindfulness training for London

Good Thinking has teamed up with Be Mindful to provide free places on the leading online mindfulness course, exclusively for the mental wellbeing of Londoners.

*Offer is only valid to London residents. Other terms and conditions may apply.

Be Mindful

Be Mindful is an online course for learning to practice mindfulness: a mind-body approach to life that helps us to relate differently to experiences

What does the course involve?

With a structure that's easy-to-follow at your own pace, you’ll be guided through all the elements of Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT). This will involve following 10 half hour training sessions and completing 12 mindfulness assignments in your daily life

How might the course help me?

By the end of the course, you'll be regularly practising mindfulness and enjoying the life-changing results the practice of mindfulness brings. For example, the course is the subject of published research studies, showing that course completers enjoy average reductions of over 50% in anxiety and depression and over 40% in stress.

With Be Mindful, the decision to begin your mindfulness journey couldn’t be simpler; it’s free, with step-by-step guidance throughout, and in as little as 4 weeks you'll be rewarded with mindfulness skills that last a lifetime!
