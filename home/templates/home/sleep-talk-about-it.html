HealthUnlocked

https://healthunlocked.com

HealthUnlocked is a social networking site helping users to connect with others who share similar health conditions. The service has over 700 online communities focused on a range of health and wellbeing topics, including sleep and anxiety.

Pros:

  • offers peer-to-peer support - connecting with others is proven to enhance wellbeing
  • safe, secure and anonymous
  • can be downloaded as an app or browsed online

Cons:

  • content can be difficult to filter
  • some threads less active than others

Mumsnet - Talking about Sleep

https://www.mumsnet.com/Talk/sleep

A discussion forum for mums and dads to talk about all things sleep related. 

Pros:

  • A well-known and trusted forum
  • Easy to use and find the specific topic you want to talk about 
  • Focused on sleep - with other subject areas available if needed 
  • Quick responses and discussion with people in similar situations
  • By parents, for parents -  but can still provide useful information for non-parents

Cons:

  • Not medical advice
  • Fairly advert heavy
  • Could be overwhelming as a lot of content

Reddit - get out of bed

https://www.reddit.com/r/GetOutOfBed
Reddit is a social and news aggregator, with digital content reviews and ratings. Users can "upvote" or "downvote" a post, with comments often becoming extended discussion forums. This sub-reddit is for discussions on 'get out of bed'.

Pros:

  • Easy to use and filter to personalise content
  • Focused on topics related to getting out of bed
  • A good guide to the most talked about topics, user advice, user reviews, and links for more information

Cons:

  • Partially moderated
  • User cliques can bury articles they don't agree with (as posts with the upvotes are given prominence over those with downvotes)
