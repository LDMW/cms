Breathe Sync

https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/breathesync/id722304193?mt=8

Breathe Sync is an app that monitors your pulse using your phone's camera to calculate the optimum breathe cycle to match the rhythm of your heart.


Pros:

  • A simple tool to check in with your breathing at times of stress or frustration
  • Inbuilt daily reminders
  • Free

Cons:

  • Accuracy of pulse reading not guaranteed 
  • Other apps have similar features plus additional tools such as guided meditations

The Mix

http://www.themix.org.uk

The Mix is an online support service providing free, confidential support for young people under 25 via online resources and phone, peer-to-peer, online and counselling services. 

Pros:

  • Free and confidential
  • A mix of online resources and immediate support
  • A way of connecting with peers through online discussion boards

Cons:

  • Telephone counselling currently unavailable
  • Limited hours for live support (helplines and one-to-one chat open 11am-11pm every day)
